About La Vie

An exclusive Brooklyn salon, with reasonable Brooklyn prices.

Welcome to La Vie — a charming hair salon and day spa on Brighton Beach Avenue. Our senior stylists specialize in every hair type and texture, and they'll guide you toward the products and routines designed for your specific hair.

We stay current with every new color technique, trend and style — from haircuts and highlights, to keratin treatments, extensions and full bridal services. La Vie is also the place for the ultimate in pampering: waxing, facials, manicures, pedicures, lashes, makeup application and wedding-day services.

The wonderful thing about a full-service salon and spa? You can enjoy spa treatments — waxing, brow and lash tinting, or a spa mani-pedi — while your color processes. We make every minute of your visit count.
